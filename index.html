My Projects

Stratospheric Composition

This project will use NASA satellite observations and a hierarchy of climate models to understand how changes in the composition of the stratosphere (e.g., ozone, water vapor, aerosols) influence the temperature structure of the stratosphere. Theses analyses will be directed to better understand the cause of temperature biases among different climate models and their impact on model projections of both contemporary and paleo climate change.
